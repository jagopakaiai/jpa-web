# .picasso.md — CIM Design Direction

## Direction: Nothing Living Billboard

A single-viewport 5-cell dashboard inspired by Nothing's industrial design language and airport departure boards. OLED black, monochrome hierarchy, dot-matrix display typography, mechanical precision. Two cells are fixed (Hero, Launch Event). Three cells are rotating "billboards" whose content cycles on a staggered 8-second auto-advance with a split-flap / character-roll transition on the label. One accent: Nothing Red — shared by the Launch Event outline and the CIM World showcase slide.

## Design Dials
- DESIGN_VARIANCE: 7 (asymmetric 5-cell layout, carousel-as-architecture)
- MOTION_INTENSITY: 6 (staggered auto-advance + character-roll + slide crossfade — mechanical, bounded)
- VISUAL_DENSITY: 4 (5 spacious cells instead of 13 cramped tiles — content reveals over time)

## Typography
- Display: Doto (variable dot-matrix, 48-96px, hero/stats only)
- Body: Space Grotesk (300/400/500, headings/descriptions)
- Data/Labels: Space Mono (400, ALL CAPS, 0.08em tracking, 13px floor)
- Billboard legibility baseline: body/desc ≥ 18px, captions ≥ 15px, labels ≥ 13px — readable from ~4 ft
- Max 3 sizes per screen, 2 weights

## Color Tokens (Dark Mode Default)
- Background: #000000 (OLED black)
- Surface: #111111
- Surface Raised: #1A1A1A
- Border: #222222 / #333333
- Text: #FFFFFF (display) / #E8E8E8 (primary) / #999999 (secondary) / #666666 (disabled)
- Accent: #D71921 (Nothing Red — CIM World tile only)
- Interactive: #5B9BF6 (links)

- Surface: #FFFDF8 (cream)
- Surface Raised: #F0EDE6 (light kraft)
- Border: #D4CFC4 / #B8B2A6 (warm stone)
- Text: #1A1714 (display) / #2C2824 (primary) / #7A7468 (secondary) / #A39D94 (disabled)

- Faint 32px engineering grid overlay at 0.03 opacity

## Layout
- Single viewport (100vh, no scroll on desktop) — preserved
- CSS Grid: 12 columns × 2 rows, 5 cells:
  - **Row 1:** Hero (6 cols, fixed) · Research spotlight (6 cols, rotating — 5 slides)
  - **Row 2:** Launch Event (3 cols, fixed) · Showcase spotlight (6 cols, rotating — 6 slides) · Touchpoint (3 cols, rotating — 3 slides)
- 8px tile gaps, 12px border-radius
- Each spotlight auto-advances every 8 s. Starts staggered: Research t=0, Showcase t+3s, Touchpoint t+5s — transitions never coincide.
- Per-spotlight controls at the bottom: segmented pill (‹ · dots · ›) + play/pause toggle. Hover/focus pauses that spotlight only. Keyboard: ←/→ cycle, space toggles pause, Home/End jump.
- Character-roll transition on every slide's label (Space Mono): glyphs cycle 3–5 times at ~45 ms each, per-character 25 ms stagger. Total ≈ 500–700 ms. Non-label elements crossfade underneath.
- `aria-live="polite"` on the stage is toggled on only around auto-advances, off for manual nav — screen readers announce rotations but aren't spammed during interaction.
- Breakpoints:
  - Desktop (>1200px): 5-cell billboard, single viewport, all 3 spotlights auto-advance
  - Tablet (769–1200px): 2-col stacked billboard, rotations still run
  - Mobile (≤768px): single flex column; auto-advance disabled, manual prev/next/dots remain
- `prefers-reduced-motion`: auto-advance halted, character-roll collapses to instant character swap, slide transitions are instant opacity toggles
- Hero title uses `clamp(56px, 8vw, display-xl)` so display stays dominant; slide display values use similar clamps so no cell truncates at any viewport

## Identity
- The surprise: three cells silently rotating content — the page is "alive" without being noisy
- Nothing Red accent: Launch Event border (urgency signal) and CIM World showcase slide (only)
- Toggle labeled "DARK" / "LIGHT" — shows the current mode
- Tertiary labels feel like instrument panels (Space Mono, ALL CAPS, tiny) and character-roll on every slide change
- Stats/numerals in Doto display font — data IS the visual
- No shadows, no gradients, no illustrations
- Flat surfaces separated by borders only

## Rejections
- No shadows or blur
- No gradients
- No purple/indigo/violet anything
- No centered vertical layout
- No uniform card grids (tiles are deliberately different sizes)
- No hover translate-y or shadow-lift effects
- No skeleton loading
- No filled icons or emoji

## Post-v3 refinements
- VISIT tile is left-aligned with a 44px venue and Space Mono caps address block — the tile's one deliberate architectural break (per plan-v3 Change 2)
- Research-row status is a filled, bordered Space Mono pill anchored at the right end of a 6px progress rule — the tile's one moment of surprise, and the only filled element on the row (per plan-v3 Change 4)
