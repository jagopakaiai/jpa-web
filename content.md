## Game Studio

*Where play becomes profession*

The Game Studio cluster will train the next generation of game developers through hands-on production, industry partnerships, and cutting-edge tools.

### Planned Projects

- **Owl Quest** — A campus-themed adventure game envisioned for Unreal Engine 5, to be built by student teams.
  `Unreal Engine` · `Level Design` · `Narrative`

- **Esports Arena** — A planned competitive gaming facility to support varsity and club teams.
  `Competition` · `Streaming` · `Community`

- **Indie Incubator** — A planned mentorship program to help students publish original games on Steam and itch.io.
  `Publishing` · `Mentorship` · `Indie Dev`

## Digital Twin of Georgia

*Modeling the Peach State, digitally*

A research cluster focused on creating high-fidelity digital replicas of Georgia infrastructure for urban planning, disaster response, and environmental monitoring.

### Planned Projects

- **Atlanta 3D** — Planned photogrammetric reconstruction of metro Atlanta for city planning simulations.
  `Photogrammetry` · `GIS` · `Urban Planning`

- **Flood Predictor** — An envisioned real-time flood simulation using LiDAR terrain data and weather APIs.
  `Simulation` · `LiDAR` · `Climate`

- **Heritage Scan** — A planned effort to digitally preserve Georgia historic sites through 3D scanning and gaussian splatting.
  `3D Scanning` · `Preservation` · `Culture`

## XR & Healthcare

*Healing through immersion*

A research cluster exploring VR/AR applications for medical training, patient therapy, and healthcare accessibility.

### Planned Projects

- **SurgSim VR** — A planned virtual reality surgical training platform with haptic feedback integration.
  `VR` · `Medical Training` · `Haptics`

- **PhobiaFree** — An envisioned graduated exposure therapy tool in VR for anxiety and phobia treatment.
  `Therapy` · `Mental Health` · `VR`

- **AR Anatomy** — A planned augmented reality anatomy visualization for nursing and pre-med students.
  `AR` · `Education` · `Anatomy`

## Digital Humanities & Arts

*Technology meets culture*

A research cluster bridging art, history, and technology through interactive exhibits, digital storytelling, and creative computing.

### Planned Projects

- **StoryWeaver** — A planned AI-assisted interactive fiction platform for creative writing courses.
  `AI` · `Narrative` · `Creative Writing`

- **Virtual Gallery** — An envisioned WebXR exhibition space to showcase student digital art and installations.
  `WebXR` · `Art` · `Exhibition`

- **Oral Histories** — A planned immersive documentary project to preserve community voices in spatial audio.

## Impact

### What We're Building Toward

The Center for Interactive Media is in its founding stage. Our mission is to create an interdisciplinary hub where students, faculty, and industry partners collaborate on applied research across games, simulation, extended reality, and digital culture.

As we grow, we aim to:

- Provide hands-on research opportunities for students across multiple disciplines
- Develop partnerships with industry leaders in gaming, XR, and simulation
- Secure external funding to support faculty and student research
- Publish and present findings at leading conferences and journals
- Build state-of-the-art facilities for immersive technology development

We're actively seeking collaborators, advisors, and partners who share our vision.
